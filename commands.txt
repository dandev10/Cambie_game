commands in menu:
1. start    -   starts the main game
2. quit     -   quits the game
3. info     -   gives the information of the game

commands in game(not direct):
1. /quit    -   quits the whole game
2. /main    -   goes to the main menu
3. /help    -   gives you list of commands
4. /stats   -   gives the player stats

commands in game(direct):
1. place   -   tells you what cordinates you are on
